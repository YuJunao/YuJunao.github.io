Research details Undergraduate Innovation Plans, Advisor：Prof. Congwen Liu

On Beckner's Inequality for Axially Symmetric Functions on S^n.

The conjecture of Beckner's inequality is a higher dimensional extension of Nirenberg problem in conformal geometry. In recent years, Prof. Changfeng Gui and Juncheng Wei have made a breakthrough when the dimension n=4,6,8 together with their groups. In this program, we aim to study their works, simplify the proof, and hopefully solve the problem in higher dimensions.

L.-K. Hua Seminar (H), Advisor：Prof. Yong Wei. Summer Research Internship at Purdue University, Advisor：Prof. Changyou Wang.
